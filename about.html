About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team behind Shanel Consultants.

Our Story

Shanel Consultants was founded with a passion for STEM education. We believe in empowering young minds to explore the wonders of science and technology. Our journey began when we realized the need for personalized tutoring programs that ignite curiosity, improve grades, and build essential STEM skills.

Our Qualifications

Our team consists of highly qualified educators with extensive experience in teaching STEM subjects. We hold advanced degrees in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, and are certified to teach at various educational levels.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help students succeed in STEM subjects by providing personalized tutoring that caters to their individual needs. We aim to inspire curiosity, foster a love for learning, and equip students with the skills they need to excel in their academic and professional careers.
